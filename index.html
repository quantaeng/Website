About Quanta Engineering Solutions

A Unit of Minion Enterprises

Quanta Engineering Solutions has extensive experience in testing electrical systems and equipment in utility facilities, including all types of substations up to 765 kV (transmission & distribution).

Our group of highly qualified engineers brings years of experience and expertise in testing, start-up, and comprehensive commissioning of electrical systems. We maintain knowledge of sustainable testing processes and procedures that ensure the highest standards of reliability and safety.

Our engineers are trained in Operation and Maintenance (O&M) during testing and start-up activities. From design to field execution, our seasoned engineering staff and recognized field test engineers are fully competent to perform complete commissioning activities including protective relay setting tests.
