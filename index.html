Our Expertise

Quanta Engineering Solutions has extensive experience in the testing of electrical systems and equipment in electrical utility facilities, including all types of substations up to 765 kV (e.g., transmission & distribution).

Our group of highly qualified engineers have years of experience and expertise in testing and start-up, and in general, commissioning of electrical systems along with knowledge of sustainable testing processes and procedures.

Our Capabilities

Our engineers are trained in Operation and Maintenance (O&M) during testing and start-up activities. Our seasoned engineering staff for design, along with a highly recognized group of field test engineers and technicians.

We are competent to perform a whole unit of commissioning activities to protective relay setting tests across various power generation facilities including GIS, AIS, Thermal, Wind, Hydro, and Solar Power Plants.

Testing Equipment & Specializations

State-of-the-art equipment for precise testing

Installation & Erection

We provide comprehensive installation and erection services including panel erection, switchyard erection, cabling, glanding, termination, transformer erection work, civil foundation work, and monopole erection works.

Design Services

Our team specializes in the design of substation automation systems and integration of protective relays to existing Substation Automation Systems (SAS), ensuring seamless operation and enhanced reliability.

Advanced Testing

We perform SFRA (Sweep Frequency Response Analysis), Tan Delta testing, DCRM testing, and various other specialized tests using state-of-the-art equipment to ensure optimal performance and safety.

Operation & Maintenance

Electrical maintenance ensures equipment is kept in good working order. We provide regular inspections, testing, and repair services, along with overhauling work for all types of electrical equipment.
